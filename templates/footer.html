Objective of SurFood

The objective of SurFood is to reduce food wastage by redistributing surplus and expiring foods. Nowaways, most of the supermarkets come with mark down zone to sell expiring items, but somehow these items are not available in online store and has a very low social exposure. SurPlus is an online portal that focusing on selling surplus and expiring foods, which allows customer to purchase expiring foods at a highly-discounted price and pick-up the items at desired store.
